About IEEE GNU

Established in 2025 at Guru Nanak University, Ibrahimpatnam, Hyderabad

Our Mission

To foster a culture of innovation and technological excellence among students at Guru Nanak University. We aim to provide a platform for students to enhance their technical skills, engage in research, and connect with industry professionals.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization that bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, preparing students for successful careers in technology and engineering fields.

What We Do

  • Organize technical workshops & seminars on emerging technologies
  • Conduct coding competitions and hackathons
  • Facilitate industry visits and internship opportunities
  • Provide access to IEEE's vast library of technical resources
  • Host guest lectures from industry experts and researchers
  • Support student research projects and paper publications
  • Create networking opportunities with professionals and alumni
